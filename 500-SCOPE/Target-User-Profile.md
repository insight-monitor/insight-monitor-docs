# Target User Profile

## Primary: Enterprise teams
Organizations with predominantly digital workflows — software engineering, data analysis, customer support, operations. Employers who need nuanced activity insights beyond binary blocking.

## Secondary: Individual users
Freelancers, knowledge workers, or anyone who wants voluntary self-monitoring for personal productivity analysis. Full data control remains with the user.

## Device distinction

### Company-owned devices
Employer can mandate minimum telemetry during working hours. Collection policy is transparent and bounded.

### Bring-your-own-device (BYOD)
User has full control over what is shared and when. No mandatory collection. Opt-in only.

## Out of scope
- Non-digital labor (construction, manufacturing, transportation)
- Roles requiring high privacy guarantees (therapy, journalism, legal defense)
- Minors under legal working age in their jurisdiction
