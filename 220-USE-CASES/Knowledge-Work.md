# Use Case: Knowledge Work

## Observable patterns
- Heavy reading: PDFs, documents, long-form articles, reports
- Writing: docs, spreadsheets, presentations
- Intermittent bursts of typing followed by long reading periods
- Multiple reference sources open simultaneously

## What the system can infer
- Reading comprehension phase (document open, low input, page scrolling)
- Writing/authoring phase (document editor with text input)
- Research gathering (tabs open, copy-paste between sources)

## Limitations
- Cannot distinguish deep analysis from skimming
- Reading a novel vs reading a report produce identical signals
- Comparing two documents side by side is invisible to screen monitoring
